About Us

Hila-Craft Interiors is a full-service residential interior design and build firm, founded by Arianne Njambi in 2006. Through her years of experience, Njambi has developed a unique style of mixing old with new and giving each project a timeless feel. Seamlessly juxtaposing limestone, marble, earthy woods, textured fabrics and organic colors with materials such as metal and glass is what gives Njambi's work a striking and harmonious feel.

Hila-Craft Interiors' philosophy is to create inviting and elegant interiors-- straying from trends that will only disappoint in years to come. Their design approach is to Architecturally space plan every square inch of a room to work both aesthetically and functionally. Each project is tailored specifically to each client, as we pay special attention to every detail that goes into project. Known for our incredible Kitchens and Bathroom renovations, challenges such as small spaces and children's rooms are welcome and embraced by us, as we creatively transform some of the smallest most unusable spaces to multi-functional rooms. Hila-Craft Interiors is committed to using sustainable materials, wherever possible.

Hila-Craft Interiors designs and furnishes homes from coast to coast. Here is the process for our Nairobi clientele.

The Process

  1. Initial Call or Meeting
  2. Planning
    3. We create a floorplan incorporating your current furniture and the new pieces that you'll need.
  3. Budget
    4. We present a budget for your approval, based on your floorplans.
  4. Selections
    5. We present furniture, fabric, art and accessory options for your approval.
  5. Rework & Pricing
    6. We present a second round of selections for anything that you did not approve in round one. At this time we will also provide pricing for all items that you've approved.
  6. Deposit & Order
    7. You provide a furnishings deposit, and we place your furnishings order.
  7. Delivery & Installation
    8. Your design team oversees the deliveries, placement and installation for your home.

Our Team

Arianne Njambi

Founder

Njambi started the company in 2006 and holds a bachelors degree in interior design from the University of Nairobi. She also earned a certificate in Universal Design from the Graduate School of Design at Harvard University and has furthered her design studies with courses toward a Masters in Architecture at JKUAT. She is an ASID allied member and has experience in a variety of aspects and disciplines of architecture and interiors. Her experience ranges from both commercial and residential spaces.
